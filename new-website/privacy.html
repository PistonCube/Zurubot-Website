Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2024

1. Data Collection

The bot does not collect personal information unless required for its operation, such as user IDs or server IDs to provide specific features.

2. Data Usage

Data collected by the bot is used solely for the purpose of providing services like command execution and server management. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

The bot stores necessary information temporarily during its runtime. Persistent data, if any, is kept securely and deleted once it is no longer needed.

4. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they generate using the bot. Inappropriate content may be reported to server administrators.

5. Data Sharing

ZuruBot does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. Only aggregated, anonymized data may be used for analytical purposes.

6. Data Security

The bot implements measures to protect data from unauthorized access, but no system is entirely secure. Users are encouraged to report any vulnerabilities.

7. Third-Party Integrations

Some features may integrate with external services (e.g., music playback or API interactions). Data shared with these services is subject to their privacy policies.

8. User Rights

Users can request the removal of their data or inquire about the data stored related to their account by contacting the bot developers.

9. Cookies and Tracking

The bot does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms. Any web-based services linked to the bot may have their own policies.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in services or regulations. Users will be notified of major changes.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this policy or to report issues, contact the Zurubot development team via our Discord server or official website.
